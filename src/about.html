G.O’S ADDRESS

Dearly Beloved,

Indeed our God has been faithful. Recently, we commenced the New Creation College of Ministry and Seminary. This is in line with our vision to train men and women of God for the work of the ministry. To the glory of God, we have 10 Seminarians on campus presently both from within the New Creation Gospel Mission fold and other ministries.

The School of Mission and Seminary has also received a great boost by the crop of Tutors and Mentors that God sent our way. These are spirit-filled men of the gospel who are happy to build the students on the holiest faith with spiritual and secular knowledge necessary for equipping the saints for the work of the gospel and the edification of the body of Christ.

THE CLARION CALL

This is a Clarion Call to all lovers of mission, corporate organizations, individual friends and associates seeking fertile ground to sow their seed for gospel outreaches to partner with us on our “Sponsor a Missionary” fundraising campaign to increase enrolment in our School of Mission and to support already enrolled students for their tuition, boarding and feeding. The School of Mission and Seminary is a fulltime and fully residential, Four semester program.

CONCLUSION

My prayer for you is that you find the courage to do that which God will lay in your heart to do in this regard.

God bless you.
