CALL FOR SPONSORSHIP

NECCOMS hereby invites you to partner with us on our “SUPPORT A MISSIONARY” PROJECT. New Creation College of Ministry and Seminary (NECCOMS) run a four(4)semester resident school program leading to the award of a Diploma in Theology and Pastoral Ministry with each semester running for a period of sixteen(16) weeks.

The “Sponsor a Missionary” project is an opportunity for individuals, corporate bodies, and gospel outreaches to partake in the joy of evangelizing the nation by directly sponsoring an individual seminarian selected through the student profile on our website or by Incognito sponsorship wherein the school is obliged to assign a beneficiary to a sponsor.

Sponsors will also have the privilege to interact with their beneficiaries (whether selected or assigned) and will get regular notification on their progress in school till graduation. Sponsors can pay monthly (₦40,000) or per semester (₦160,000). Payment can also be made generously as the Spirit leads.
